About Me

Alfonso Diaz Celito

I am a front-end web developer with about 5 years of experience. I work with personal and academic projects to create high-performing, interactive websites that work in most web browsers and on all devices.

Although I am very familiar with new technologies and with content management systems (CMS), my websites are mainly hand coded with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and SVG images.

With a strong emphasis on "Progressive Enhancement", I look for creative ways to exploit the website's front-end code without compromising browser performance.

In a quest to always stay up-to-date, I like to attend programming events such as Hackathons. In addition, I like to join communities on different platforms where experiences are shared and one learns from other people to improve every day.
